Product Details

Sugarcane is pressed into water and then packed in plastic bags and frozen. Frozen sugarcane juice has a shelf life of up to 2 years when stored at a temperature of minus 18 degrees Celsius.

In sugarcane juice, there are saccharos, calcium, chromium, cobalt, copper, magnesium, manganese, phosphorus, potassium and zinc.

It also provides iron and vitamins A, C, B1, B2, B3, B5, B6 and many phytonutrient, antioxidant, protein and other soluble fiber needed for the body. Thanks to these ingredients, sugarcane juice has a lot of good health.

The process of Frozen Sugarcane Juice

The sugar cane is washed, peeled and washed before being pressed for water

Sugarcane juice is packed and frozen at a negative temperature of 40-35 degrees C
